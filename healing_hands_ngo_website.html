Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is Healing Hands an NGO?

Healing Hands is a humanitarian and animal welfare initiative founded to serve with honesty and compassion. Formal NGO registration is planned as the initiative grows.

How are donations used?

Donations are used directly for helping people in need, animal care, food, basic treatment, and community support. We believe in transparency and responsible use of every contribution.

Can I help without donating?

Yes, absolutely. You can help by feeding animals, helping someone in need, volunteering your time or skills, sharing positive stories, and spreading kindness in daily life.

Do you work only with animals or also with people?

Healing Hands works for both humanity and animal welfare. We believe every life deserves care, dignity, and compassion.
